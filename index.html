About Me

Hi! I am a Bachelor of Multimedia Arts student who loves turning ideas into visuals through design, animation, and digital media. Whether it is creating posters, editing videos, or exploring motion graphics, I enjoy using creativity and technology to tell meaningful stories and connect with people. I'm always learning, experimenting with new tools, and pushing my skills to grow as a versatile and passionate digital artist.

Background

Education

I am currently studying Multimedia Arts at University of the Cordilleras, where I am honing my skills in traditional and digital art, animation, graphic design, and 3D modeling. My education allows me to explore different creative disciplines and tools, helping me grow as a versatile artist capable of bringing stories to life through various visual mediums.

Interests

Outside of academics, I love spending time drawing, editing videos, and experimenting with different art styles. I enjoy watching animated films, listening to soundtracks, and discovering artists that inspire me. I am always curious about how things are made and love diving into behind-the-scenes content or tutorials to learn new creative techniques.

Works

My works reflect a combination of traditional and digital practices. I often begin with hand-drawn concepts, which evolve into digital pieces or animated scenes. I have explored 2D and 3D animation, character and environment design, and visual storytelling through both static and moving images. I am constantly refining my process and pushing my creativity in every project I take on.
